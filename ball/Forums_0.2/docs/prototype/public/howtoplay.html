Playing is easy. Just follow these steps:

  1. Download and install The Ball.
  2. Once you've installed The Ball, you'll be able to access it through your Internet browser (Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox).
  3. Check out the list of active games. You can evaluate them by when they started, what kind of prizes they have to offer, or by who is currently leading.
  4. When you find a game you like, just register for it. Now you're in the game, and it will appear in your list of "active games."
  5. Follow the first clue, which will point you to a location on the Web.
  6. Look around &8211; click a few links and explore a few pages, and you'll eventually find a clue.
  7. One problem, though: the clue doesn't want to be found. To access it, you'll need to solve a puzzle or a brainteaser &8211; it could be a scrambled word or image, a "hangman" game, a jigsaw puzzle, or other variations.
  8. Once you've solved the puzzle, the clue will give you some useful information. It could be a tip on where The Ball is rolling next, or it could be one of the virtual keys you'll need to unlock The Ball when you find it.
Good hunting!
