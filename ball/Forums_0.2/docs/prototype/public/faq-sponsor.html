  1. What are the benefits of sponsorship?
    The primary benefit of hosting The Ball is increased traffic to your Web site. And unlike other forms of Web advertising, hosting The Ball guarantees that visitors will actually pay attention to your content. Simply put, they have to -- to win, players need to identify specific keywords and phrases on your site in order to progress to the next clue.

    In addition, your site gets the positive, brand-building buzz of association with a new, innovative and fun way to explore the Web.
     
  2. What does it take to get verified as a Sponsor?
    Before you can sponsor The Ball on your Web site, we will verify that you are either the owner of the URL domain on whose behalf you are bidding, or that you are bidding on behalf of (and with the permission of) that owner. We may need to contact you to finalize this verification process – to help this process go as smoothly as possible, please ensure that the contact information in your profile is up-to-date and accurate.
     
  3. How does the Sponsorship Auction work?
    Sponsorship Auctions follow a standard auction format. During the course of the auction, you can choose to enter manual bids (with a minimum bid $5 higher than the current winning bid), or you can set a maximum bid and allow the system to automatically raise your bids as needed.
