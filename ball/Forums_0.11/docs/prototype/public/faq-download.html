  1. How do I install The Ball for Internet Explorer?
    To install the Google Toolbar for Internet Explorer, you must have Microsoft Windows 2000/XP/2003 and Microsoft Internet Explorer 5.5 and above. To install The Ball in Internet Explorer, follow these steps:

    1. Open a Microsoft Internet Explorer window.
    2. Visit www.theball.com, go to the “Download” page and click the "Internet Explorer" button.
    3. When you see the next screen, choose "Open" or "Run."
    4. Review the Terms of Use, and choose "Agree."
    5. Continue through the rest of the installation. When the installation is complete, The Ball plug-in will be incorporated at the top of your Internet Explorer screen.
     
  2. How do I install The Ball for Mozilla Firefox?
    To install The Ball for Firefox, follow these steps:

    1. Open a Mozilla Firefox window
    2. Visit www.theball.com, go to the “Download” page and click the "Mozilla Firefox" button.
    3. Review the Terms of Use and click "Install."
    4. A new window should open (if it doesn’t, see below). In this window, click "Install The Ball."
    5. Continue through the rest of the installation.
    6. When installation is complete, close all your Firefox browsers and windows. When you restart it, The Ball should appear at the top of your Firefox screen.

    If a new window doesn't open when you click "Install," the installation was probably blocked. At the top of your screen, you should see a yellow bar that says "To protect your computer, Firefox prevented this site from installing software on your computer." To install The Ball, you will need to allow downloads from our site:

    1. Click the "Edit Options..." button in the yellow bar.
    2. The Firefox "Allowed Sites" window will appear.
    3. The Ball address should automatically appear; if not, enter “www.theball.com” and click "Allow."
    4. Click "OK."
    5. Click "Install" again to restart installation.
     
  3. How do I uninstall The Ball?
    1. Click on the "Settings" link in The Ball plug-in.
    2. Choose "Help" and then "Uninstall."

    You can also uninstall the Toolbar through Windows. Click on the Windows "Start" menu, then select "Settings" > "Control Panel" > "Add or Remove Programs." Find "The Ball plug-in " in the list and click the "Remove" button.
     
  4. I installed The Ball in Internet Explorer -- why won't it show up in Firefox (or vice versa)?
    The Ball is available for both Microsoft Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox. However, you must install it separately for each browser.
