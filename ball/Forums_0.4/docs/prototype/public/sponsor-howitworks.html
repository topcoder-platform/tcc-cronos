The Ball is a little like baseball -- there’s no clock, so the game takes just as long as it takes for players to pursue clues, solve puzzles, and track The Ball across the Web.

The analogy works for sponsors, too. Each game is composed of a number of blocks, which are sort of like innings in a baseball game – you don’t know when exactly they’ll happen, just that you’ll get your turn.

Within each block is a number of slots. In baseball terms, these would correspond to advertising slots within a particular inning. When your slot comes up – which is governed by the progress of the game, and a few randomizing factors we’ve built in to eliminate cheating – The Ball will be “hosted” on your Web site, and players will swarm all over it.

To sponsor a game, you must register to become a sponsor. Once you’re verified, you can join in our Sponsorship Auction.
